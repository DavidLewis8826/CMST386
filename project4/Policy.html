80 Hour Policy/ Do Not Move Policy

This policy states anytime an associate is trained in to a new path they must then spend a total of eighty hours in that path. For associate to be excluded they must get L7 approval and there be a bussiness need. These needs could be low work in path or sev 1 or 2 events. Either way eventally the associate should reach eighty hours in the new path learned.
